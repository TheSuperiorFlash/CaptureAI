Screenshot any question. Get the exact answer.

CaptureAI is the fastest way to get answers while studying online. Select any question on your screen — from multiple choice to complex math — and get an accurate, AI-powered answer in seconds. No copy-pasting, no switching tabs, no hassle.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HOW IT WORKS

1️⃣ Capture — Press Ctrl+Shift+X or click the extension icon to start a screenshot selection. Drag over any question on your screen.

2️⃣ Process — CaptureAI uses advanced OCR to extract the text from your screenshot. If text confidence is high, only the text is sent (saving bandwidth). Otherwise, the image is analyzed directly.

3️⃣ Answer — AI analyzes the question and returns a clear, accurate answer in a floating panel right on your page. No tab switching required.

KEY FEATURES

🎯 Floating Answer Panel — A sleek, draggable panel appears right on your page with the AI's answer. No pop-ups, no new tabs. Resize, reposition, or minimize it to fit your workflow.

👻 Stealth Mode — Answers appear as barely-visible text in the corner of your screen. Only you can see them. Perfect for when you need discretion.

🔍 Smart OCR — Powered by Tesseract.js, CaptureAI extracts text from screenshots with high accuracy. When confidence is above 60%, only text is sent to the AI — reducing tokens by up to 90% for faster responses.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts — Work without touching the mouse:
  • Ctrl+Shift+X — Start a new capture
  • Ctrl+Shift+F — Quick recapture (reuses your last selection area)
  • Ctrl+Shift+E — Toggle the answer panel

🧠 Multiple AI Models — Choose your reasoning level:
  • Fast mode (gpt-4.1-nano) — Instant answers, no reasoning
  • Default (gpt-5-nano, low reasoning) — Balanced speed and accuracy
  • Best quality (gpt-5-nano, medium reasoning) — Pro only, most accurate
