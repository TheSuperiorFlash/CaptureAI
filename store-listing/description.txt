CaptureAI is the fastest way to get answers while studying online. Select any question on your screen, from multiple choice to complex math, and get an accurate, AI-powered answer in seconds. No copy-pasting, no switching tabs.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Capture — Drag to select any question on your screen.

2. Read — CaptureAI reads the question from your selection automatically.

3. Answer — AI analyzes the question and returns a clear, accurate answer in a floating panel right on your page.

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS

Works on every major learning platform:
• Canvas (Instructure)
• Moodle
• Blackboard
• Top Hat
• Schoology
• Respondus
• Google Forms, Microsoft Forms
• And virtually any website with questions

KEY FEATURES

Floating Answer Panel — A draggable panel appears right on your page with the answer. No pop-ups, no new tabs. Reposition, or hide it when needed.

Stealth Mode — Answers appear as barely-visible text in the corner of your screen. Only you can see them.

Keyboard Shortcuts — Work without touching the mouse:
  • Ctrl+Shift+X — Start a new capture
  • Ctrl+Shift+F — Quick recapture (reuses your last selection)
  • Ctrl+Shift+E — Toggle the answer panel
